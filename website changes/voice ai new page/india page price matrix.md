**Q: Will my callers trust it?**

Most callers don't ask. They just want their question answered and their appointment booked. When they do ask "am I speaking to a person?", it answers honestly. It doesn't claim to be human.

What builds trust isn't whether it's human. It's whether it answered the question, booked the slot, and didn't waste the caller's time. The AI does all three.

**Q: What if it says something wrong?**

It never makes up an answer. If it doesn't know something, it says "let me have the team follow up with you." It takes the message, logs it, and sends your team a WhatsApp alert immediately.
